Dr. Téo Sanchez is a postdoctoral researcher and computer scientist focusing on human-AI interaction and the artistic communities of practice that form around AI technologies. His research ranges from the early AI artists who engaged with deep learning in the late 2010s to today's recreational online communities centered on text-to-image generation. From 2023 to 2024, he was a lecturer and academic contributor at the AICA Digitalization College. Trained at IRCAM Paris, he is currently a Marie Skłodowska-Curie Fellow at the "Munich Interactive Intelligence Initiative" at LMU Munich under Prof. Ophelia Deroy, as well as a member of the research initiative "Regaining Power over AI," in collaboration with Kate Crawford, Gonzalo Ramos (Microsoft Research), and Baptiste Caramiaux (CNRS).
